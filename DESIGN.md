# Design System — Jack爸爸学数学

## Product Context
- **What this is:** 打印纸玩具电商网站
- **Who it's for:** 小学生家长
- **Space/industry:** 教育玩具 / Print-on-Demand
- **Project type:** 电商网站

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Direction:** Print.com 风格 - 专业简洁
- **Decoration level:** intentional - 简洁但有质感
- **Mood:** 专业可信赖，同时活泼友好
- **Reference sites:** print.com

## Typography
- **Display/Hero:** Noto Serif SC (思源宋体) - 中文标题
- **Body:** Noto Sans SC (思源黑体) - 中文正文
- **UI/Labels:** 同 Body
- **Loading:** Google Fonts CDN

## Color
- **Approach:** balanced - 蓝色为主，金色点缀
- **Primary:** #1E40AF - 深蓝色，主按钮/强调
- **Primary-light:** #3B82F6 - 浅蓝，hover状态
- **Secondary:** #F59E0B - 金色，活跃气氛
- **Secondary-light:** #FBBF24 - 浅金
- **Background-white:** #FFFFFF
- **Background-light:** #F8FAFC
- **Background-gray:** #F1F5F9
- **Text-dark:** #1E293B
- **Text-gray:** #64748B
- **Text-light:** #94A3B8
- **Success:** #10B981
- **Warning:** #F59E0B
- **Error:** #EF4444

## Spacing
- **Base unit:** 8px
- **Density:** comfortable
- **Scale:** 2xs(4) xs(8) sm(12) md(16) lg(24) xl(32) 2xl(48) 3xl(64)

## Layout
- **Approach:** grid-disciplined - 整齐的网格布局
- **Grid:** 12列，移动端响应式
- **Max content width:** 1200px
- **Border radius:** sm:4px, md:8px, lg:12px, xl:16px

## Motion
- **Approach:** minimal-functional
- **Easing:** ease-out
- **Duration:** micro(100ms), short(200ms), medium(300ms)
